Workshop Details

  • BRAC University Film Club hosting a Scriptwriting Workshop featuring Ayman Asib Shadhin, the renowned scriptwriter of Utshob, Kaiser and Mainkar Chipay. The session is designed to enhance students' understanding of storytelling, screenplay structure and creative writing for film and television.

    This workshop will be open to all BRAC University students who are interested in film, literature or creative writing, regardless of departmental affiliation.

    Objectives:
    1. To introduce participants to the fundamentals of professional scriptwriting.
    2. To explore the creative process behind acclaimed Bangladeshi productions.
    3. To encourage student engagement in creative writing and film development.

    Session Outline:
    1. Comprehensive discussion on developing a script, from concept to final draft.
    2. Exploration of different genres and their unique narrative structures.
    3. To encourage student engagement in creative writing and film development.
    4. Interactive Q&A session with the guest speaker.

    We believe this workshop will offer students a valuable opportunity to learn from one of Bangladesh's most respected screenwriters and gain insights into the art of scriptwriting and cinematic storytelling.
